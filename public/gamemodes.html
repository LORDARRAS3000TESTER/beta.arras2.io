D-DAY D-Day: The allies, which are blue, have more tanks than the Axis powers, which are red.
For the allies to win, they must invade and destroy the red central base on the bottom right, which is unlocked by destroying all of their dominators scattered on the map.
For the Axis powers to win, they must guard all of their dominators and base and keep at least 4 of them continuously for 8 minutes.

BOSS RUSHBoss Rush: You're all on the same team and you will have to kill the bosses that spawn to kill all your 9 sanctuaries! There are 100 waves and each waves bosses spawn and if you pass the 100th wave, you win the game! If all of your sanctuaries are dead by the bosses, there will be a timer of 90 seconds to let you recover at least 1 of your sanctuary to respawn back. You will not be able to respawn once you die until one of your team recovers at least 1 of the sanctuaries. If the 90 seconds are passed and you haven't recovered 1 of the sanctuaries, you lose the game! To make the gamemode easier and fun, there are some eggs that spawn and if they die, a friendly boss spawn and join your team! There are 2 types of eggs: Mysterious Egg and Ultra Mysterious Egg. It will spawn 5 mysterious eggs at the same time on a random wave and 3 ultra mysterious egg on a random wave!
