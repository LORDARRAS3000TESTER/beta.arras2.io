Rules of Arras2.io

Cheating Scripts Usage

Players found using cheating scripts will receive a temporary ban, which resets after the next server restart.

Chat Filter Bypass

Attempting to bypass the chat filter will result in a kick, with the possibility of a ban depending on the frequency of the offense.

VPN Detection Bypass

  • If you are banned and attempt to connect using a VPN, you will be banned again (the duration of the ban may vary).
  • If you are not banned but use a VPN, you will be kicked from the server.

Game Integrity

  • Cheating Mods: Use of any unauthorized mods or tools to gain an advantage is prohibited and will result in a temporary ban.

Appeals

  • Ban Appeals: Players who feel they have been unjustly banned can claim a ticket on the discord server, but repeated appeals after being denied may lead to further punishment.

Changes to Rules

These rules may be modified at any time.

Breaking all those rules can lead to permanent ban.
