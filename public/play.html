Changelog
Last updated: October 24th 2024

  • Added new gamemode! Core!

  • On core gamemode, a yellow zone is at the center of the map

  • To win, a team must stay on the yellow zone for longest time

  • If other teams have more players on yellow zone than your team, you can't gain points.

  • Many maps have been made for this gamemode!

  • Added new gamemodes! FFA, Maze, 2TDM and 4TDM!

  • In FFA, you fight against other players in order to have the highest score!

  • In Maze, there are some walls and it's a free for all labyrinth!

  • Just like FFA, in maze you fight against other players and gain highest score as possible

  • In 2TDM, there are 2 teams and each team fight against each other.

  • Just like FFA, in 2tdm, fight against your opponents and gain the highest score you can get!

  • In 4TDM, there are 4 teams instead of 2 like 2TDM

  • Added new bosses for boss rush mode. Also added a star which when you collect it, you gain extra powers.

  • Added new maps for boss rush and domination.

  • Added new class: Sentinel with many upgrades for Tier 2! Tier 2 only available at level 30.

  • Incoming updates:

  • Mobile support.

  • New boss rush bosses.

  • New dominators.

  • Achievements.

  • Genesis (Inspired by arras.io)

  • Hole gamemode (Inspired by hole.io)
